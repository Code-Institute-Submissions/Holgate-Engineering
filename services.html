Our Services

Repairs

We can carry out repairs on any commercial, agricultural or domestic machinery, fixtures or fittings. Repairs can be carried out insitu if required.


Prices
Prices for repairs start from as little as £5.00. A quick weld or joining will cost between £5.00 & £10.00. Large repairs of multiple pieces start from £100.00.

Refurbishments

Refurbishments can be carried out on commercial or agricutlural machinery. We can also provide refurbishment services for classic and modern car parts.


Prices
Prices for refurbishments start from £10.00. The refurbishment of a small car or machine part would usually cost between £10 & £50. Full machine refurbishments start from £60 and increase with the size of the machinery.

Manufacturing

Big or small, we offer manufacturing for any job. From one-off parts or prototypes to batch production we can cater for everyone. For batch production, we offer a prototype as standard and can produce batches of up to 3000 pieces


Prices
Manufacturing of a one piece design can start from £25.00 depending on the size and detail required. Batch production can be offered from between 10 and 3000 pieces.

Bespoke Commissions

We can make just about anything - just ask! Whether it be a classic car part no longer in circulation or just an attractive pergola for your garden. We provide consultations to help bring your vision into fruition. A few of our recent bespokes include intricate railings & gates and even a bird table.


Prices
A small bespoke creation such as a bird table would start from £30.00 however a full set of railings and gates start from £200.00.

Design

Our design engineer can transform your ideas into a clear CAD design, just let us know your specifications and we will provide you with a CAD design ready to be produced.


Prices
For large bespoke commissions and batch production, we offer a free CAD design consultation for your project. If you require a CAD design for a small project, prices start from £10.00.

Machinery

We use a large variety of machinery which allows us to cater for all requests. We have a centre laithe for large jobs and we also provide the following: Milling, Grinding, Welding (MIG, TIG & electric) & drilling.


Prices
Depending on the machinery required for a job, our charges for each machine vary. A small weld or the smoothing of a part will cost as little as £5.00.
