Our Services

Repairs

We can carry out repairs on any commercial, agricultural or domestic machinery, fixtures or fittings. Repairs can be carried out insitu if required.

Refurbishments

Refurbishments can be carried out on commercial or agricutlural machinery. We can also provide refurbishment services for classic and modern car parts.

Manufacturing

Big or small, we offer manufacturing for any job. From one-off parts or prototypes to batch production we can cater for everyone.

Bespoke Commissions

We can make just about anything - just ask! Whether it be a classic car part no longer in circulation or just an attractive pergola for your garden. We provide consultations to help bring your vision into fruition. A few of our recent bespokes include intricate railings & gates and even a bird table.

Design

CAD designs created by our design engineer, just let us know your speifications and we will provide you with a clear design.

Machinery

We use a large variety of machinery which allows us to cater for all requests. We have a centre laithe for large jobs and we also provide the following: Milling, Grinding, Welding (MIG, TIG & electric) & drilling.
